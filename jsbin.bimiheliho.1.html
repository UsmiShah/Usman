Chicken Tandori

The Recepie of the "Chicken Tandori" is given below:

Ingredients:

  • Chicken
    • 1kg
  • Yogurt
    • 5tbs
  • Turmeric Powder
    • 2tbs
  • Coriander Seeds
    • 1tbs
  • Black Pepper
    • 2tbs
  • Red Chilli Powder
    • 1tbs
  • Ginger Paste
    • 1tbs
  • Garlic Paste
    • 1tbs
  • Salt
    • As needed
  • Charcoal
    • For Smoke

Preparation Steps:

  1. Take 1kg chicken and throughly wash it.
  2. Add 5tbs Yogurt in chicken and mix it.
  3. Add Turmeric powder and coriander seeds.
  4. After this add black pepper and red chilli powder.
  5. Now we'll add Ginger and Garlic Paste
  6. Then add Salt as needed.
  7. After this Refrigerate chicken for 1 or 2 hours.
  8. Now add 2tbs oil on Pan.
  9. Add chicken in the oil and cook for half hour by chainging sides.
  10. When chicken turned brown then give the smoke of charcoal for two minutes.
  11. Now Finally, your Chicken Tandori is ready to serve
